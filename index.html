Heart Attack Risk Prediction Analysis

Heart attacks, or myocardial infarctions, continue to be a significant global health issue, necessitating a deeper comprehension of their precursors and potential mitigating factors. By employing predictive analytics on the patient's dataset encapsulating a wide range of attributes including age, cholesterol levels, blood pressure, smoking habits and more can be used to elucidate the complex interplay of these variables in determining the likelihood of a heart attack which can further help researchers and healthcare professionals to work towards proactive strategies for heart disease prevention and management. This data analysis project analyses the data and predicts the risk of Heart Attack for individuals.
